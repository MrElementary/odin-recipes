Pizza recipe

Panarottis Special

Above is my image of the pizza I will be discussing in my recipe. It originates from a local restaurant franchise here in South Africa called Panarottis and the name of the pizza itself is quite aptly named, the "Pannarottis Special".

The pizza has been my favourite go to for a few years now since it's inception at the franchise, and although it breaks the societal rule of not accepting pineapple on pizza, I believe the strength of this recipe is more than made up for in it's use of calmatta olives and salami.

Ingredients:

  • Base:
    • Flour
    • Yeast
    • Water
    • Salt
    • Olive oil
  • Tomato Paste basting
  • Salami
  • Ham
  • Mushrooms
  • Pineapple
  • Calamata Olives
  • Cheddar
  • Mozzerella
  • Garlic
  1. Make The dough/Base
  2. Proof the dough
  3. Prepare the toppings and the basting
  4. Shape the dough into a round pizza shape, usually 30cm/12inches
  5. Spread the basting and place toppings evenly across the base
  6. Bake Pizza at around 250c/450F for about 5-10minutes depending on if you're using gas or electric
  7. Take it out, let it cool down, and enjoy!
